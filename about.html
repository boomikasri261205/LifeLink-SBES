About LifeLink SBES

LifeLink – Smart Blood Emergency System (SBES) is an innovative platform designed to connect blood donors, patients, hospitals and blood banks in one place.

Our mission is to reduce the time required to find blood during emergencies by using modern technology and a smart donor network.

The system helps users search for donors, request blood, locate nearby hospitals, and improve communication during critical situations.
